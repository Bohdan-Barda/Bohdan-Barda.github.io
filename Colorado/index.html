Welcome to Colorado Home Leasing, Denver’s leading provider of lease-to-own solutions for families who want to purchase a home.

Has the bank turned you down for a mortgage?

Have you decided to delay your purchase decision but don’t want to rent without an option to participate in the housing recovery?

You’re not alone.

Millions of creditworthy Americans, who should easily qualify for a mortgage, are being denied mortgages and the opportunity to participate in the housing recovery.

Colorado Home Leasing can help.

Unlike most home rental programs, you don’t need to settle on a rental that’s “almost perfect” in a neighbor- hood that’s “acceptable.” With Colorado Home Leasing, you work with a licensed Colorado Real Estate Broker to select the perfect home in the neighborhood that’s best for you and your family. We’ll purchase the home you select, lease it to you, and provide you with opportunities to improve and purchase the home, if you choose.
